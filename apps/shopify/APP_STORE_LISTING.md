#### How It Works

1. **Install the app** and enter your OrderFlow restaurant slug
2. **Add the ordering block** to any page using the theme editor
3. **Customers order and pay** directly on your website
4. **Orders print automatically** in your kitchen via our desktop printer app

#### Features

- **Inline Menu Embed** — Full ordering menu displayed on any page of your Shopify store
- **Floating Order Button** — "Order Online" popup accessible from every page
- **Mobile Optimised** — Responsive ordering experience on all devices
- **Card Payments** — Secure checkout via Stripe, paid directly to your bank account
- **Order Sync** — Orders appear as draft orders in Shopify for accounting
- **Kitchen Printing** — Automatic receipt printing via OrderFlow's desktop agent
- **Loyalty Program** — Built-in stamp/points loyalty to bring customers back
- **Real-time Tracking** — Customers track order status from confirmation to delivery

## Installation Instructions

### For Restaurant Owners

1. Click **Install** in the Shopify App Store
2. Approve the permissions (we need: read products, content, orders)
3. Enter your OrderFlow restaurant slug in the app settings
4. Go to your **Theme Editor** → **Add section** → Choose either:
   - **OrderFlow Menu** — embeds your full ordering menu on a page
   - **OrderFlow Button** — adds a floating order button on every page
5. Done! Customers can now order from your Shopify store
